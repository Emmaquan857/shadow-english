尚雯婕 Method
Train English into reflex
Not understanding English — making it instinctive through intense dictation & shadowing.
Core Philosophy
Language is not learned — it's trained into reflex
Through high-intensity dictation, shadowing, and recitation, you build neural pathways for language. Not "understanding" English, but making it instinctive.
10
Pieces
10
Months
4
Steps
01
🎧
Blind Dictation
Listen repeatedly without the script and transcribe everything you hear. Sentence by sentence, leave blanks, repeat until you can extract no more.
02
📖
Verify & Analyze
Compare with the transcript and analyze every gap. Correct in red, study linking, vocab, and speed patterns.
03
🗣️
Shadow & Record
Repeat 50-100 times until words flow effortlessly. Mimic intonation, shadow without text, record and compare.
04
⚡
Speed Match
Recite from memory at native speed. Match pronunciation, tone, and pauses exactly. Words flow off the tongue.
📅10-Month Roadmap
M1-2
Foundation · Precision Listening
3-5 min short materials (TED-Ed, VOA Slow). Focus on dictation accuracy > 80%. 1-2 hrs daily.
Slow
M3-4
Intonation · Voice Shaping
5-8 min standard-speed materials (TED, BBC). Shift focus from dictation to shadowing quality.
Standard
M5-7
Full Length · 20 min
Full 20-min material. Complete 4-step cycle. ~1 month per piece.
Full
M8-10
Diversity · Real World
Varied accents & domains. Complete 10 pieces total — fluent comprehension & expression.
Varied
